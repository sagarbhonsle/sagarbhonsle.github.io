SAP FICO Consultant – Job Description

Overview

We are seeking an experienced SAP FICO Consultant with deep S/4HANA expertise and proven hands-on experience in configuration, customization, and full-cycle implementations. The consultant will drive core finance and controlling processes, ensure seamless integration with cross-functional SAP modules, and support business-critical operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead SAP FICO configuration and implementation activities within S/4HANA environments.
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather requirements and design Finance and Controlling solutions.
  • Configure and support key FICO components, including:
    • General Ledger (GL)
    • Accounts Payable (AP) / Accounts Receivable (AR)
    • Asset Accounting (AA)
    • Controlling (CO)
    • Profit Center & Cost Center Accounting
  • Drive testing phases (UT/SIT/UAT) and manage deployments.
  • Ensure integration of FICO with SD, MM, PP, and other SAP modules.
  • Support full-cycle implementation: blueprinting, realization, testing, go-live, and hypercare.
  • Troubleshoot FICO-related production issues and recommend process improvements.
  • Prepare high-quality functional specifications for enhancements and custom developments.

Required Skills

  • 7+ years of SAP FICO experience with strong exposure to S/4HANA.
  • Hands-on experience in configuration, customization, and end-to-end implementation.
  • Strong understanding of Finance and Controlling business processes.
  • Experience integrating FICO with SD, MM, and other SAP modules.
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and mentor junior consultants.
