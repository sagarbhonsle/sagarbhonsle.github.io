SAP MM Consultant – Job Description

Overview

We are looking for an experienced SAP MM Consultant with strong S/4HANA expertise, hands-on configuration experience, and proven end-to-end implementation background. The consultant will manage procurement, inventory, and material management processes while ensuring seamless integration with other SAP modules.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead SAP MM configuration and implementation activities in S/4HANA.
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand procurement and inventory requirements.
  • Configure and support key MM processes including:
    • Material Master and Vendor Master Data
    • Procurement (PRs, POs)
    • Inventory Management & Goods Movements
    • Valuation & Account Determination
    • Release Strategies and Approval Workflows
  • Integrate MM with SD, PP, FICO, and EWM modules.
  • Execute UT, SIT, and UAT testing cycles and manage deployments.
  • Support full-cycle implementations: blueprinting, realization, go-live, and hypercare.
  • Troubleshoot MM-related production issues and recommend process improvements.
  • Prepare functional specifications for custom developments and enhancements.

Required Skills

  • 7+ years of hands-on SAP MM experience in S/4HANA environments.
  • Strong expertise in configuration, customization, and implementation.
  • Deep understanding of procurement, inventory, and material management processes.
  • Experience integrating MM with SD, PP, FICO, and EWM.
  • Proven exposure to end-to-end full-cycle implementation projects.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication capabilities.
