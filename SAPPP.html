SAP PP Consultant – Job Description

Overview

We are seeking an experienced SAP PP Consultant with strong hands-on experience in S/4HANA, configuration, customization, and end-to-end implementation. The ideal candidate should be capable of independently managing production planning processes, collaborating with business teams, and delivering robust system solutions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead SAP PP configuration activities in S/4HANA environments.
  • Work closely with business stakeholders to understand planning and manufacturing requirements.
  • Deliver end-to-end Production Planning solutions including:
    • MRP
    • Master Data
    • Production Orders
    • Work Centers
    • Routings
    • Capacity Planning
  • Perform configuration, testing, documentation, and deployment.
  • Manage integration with QM, MM, SD, PM, and FI/CO modules.
  • Support the full implementation lifecycle: requirement gathering, blueprint, realization, testing, go-live, and hypercare.
  • Troubleshoot PP-related production issues and drive process improvements.
  • Prepare functional specifications for enhancements and custom developments.

Required Skills

  • 7+ years of hands-on SAP PP experience.
  • Strong exposure to S/4HANA PP processes and best practices.
  • Proven end-to-end implementation experience (minimum 1–2 full cycles).
  • Deep knowledge of configuration and customization within the PP module.
  • Strong understanding of Production Planning, MRP, Capacity Planning, and Shop Floor Execution.
  • Experience with integration across MM, QM, SD, PM, and CO.
  • Ability to collaborate with business users and translate requirements into SAP solutions.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
