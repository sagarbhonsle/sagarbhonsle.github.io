SAP SD Consultant – Job Description

Overview

We are looking for an experienced SAP SD Consultant with strong S/4HANA exposure and deep hands-on expertise in configuration, customization, and end-to-end implementation. The ideal candidate should be capable of independently working with business users, gathering requirements, designing solutions, and driving continuous improvements.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and deliver SAP SD configurations within S/4HANA environments.
  • Work closely with business stakeholders to gather functional requirements and translate them into robust system solutions.
  • Perform end-to-end SD module activities including:
    • Pricing
    • Order-to-Cash (OTC)
    • Billing & Invoicing
    • Delivery Processing
    • Credit Management
    • Master Data (Customer/Material)
  • Execute configuration, testing (UT/SIT/UAT), and production deployments.
  • Manage integration with MM, FI/CO, PP, and TM modules.
  • Handle enhancement requests, change management, and continuous system improvements.
  • Support full-cycle implementation phases including blueprinting, realization, testing, go-live, and hypercare.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve SAP SD-related production issues.

Required Skills

  • 7+ years of hands-on SAP SD experience.
  • Strong expertise in S/4HANA Sales & Distribution processes.
  • Proven experience in at least 1–2 full-cycle implementations.
  • Deep knowledge of SD configuration and customization.
  • Strong understanding of end-to-end OTC flows.
  • Familiarity with integration touchpoints across MM, FI, PP, and WM/EWM.
  • Ability to prepare functional specifications for ABAP developments.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving capabilities.
