Job Description – SAP MDG Consultant

Overview

We are looking for an experienced SAP MDG Consultant with hands-on S/4HANA experience and strong expertise in master data governance processes, configuration, and implementation. The consultant will ensure accurate and consistent master data across the enterprise and support overall data quality and compliance initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Configure and implement SAP MDG solutions for Material, Customer, Vendor, and other master data objects.
  • Conduct workshops with business users to gather master data requirements and define governance rules.
  • Implement validations, workflows, data replication, and approval processes.
  • Perform data quality checks, cleansing, and migration activities.
  • Integrate MDG with SAP modules such as FI, MM, SD, PP, and S/4HANA.
  • Support full-cycle project implementation: blueprinting, realization, testing, go-live, and hypercare.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve MDG-related production issues.
  • Prepare functional specifications for enhancements and custom developments.

Required Skills

  • 7+ years of SAP MDG experience in S/4HANA environments.
  • Strong hands-on experience in configuration, customization, and implementation.
  • Deep understanding of master data domains (Material, Customer, Vendor).
  • Experience working with workflows, validations, derivations, and data replication.
  • Familiarity with UI modeling, Fiori apps, and data quality tools.
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
